pressure_water_from_humidity¶

pycraf.atm.pressure_water_from_humidity(temp, press, humidity, wet_type='water')[source] [edit on github]¶

Water pressure according to ITU-R P.453-12.

Parameters:

temp : Quantity

Air temperature [K]

press : Quantity

Total air pressure [hPa]

humidity : Quantity

Relative humidity [%]

wet_type : str, optional

Type of wet material: ‘water’, ‘ice’
Returns:

press_w : Quantity

Water vapor partial pressure [hPa]
