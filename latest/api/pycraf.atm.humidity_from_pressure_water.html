humidity_from_pressure_water¶

pycraf.atm.humidity_from_pressure_water(temp, press, press_w, wet_type='water')[source] [edit on github]¶

Relative humidity according to ITU-R P.453-12.

wet_type - ‘water’ or ‘ice’

Parameters:

temp : Quantity

Air temperature [K]

press : Quantity

Total air pressure [hPa]

press_w : Quantity

Water vapor partial pressure [hPa]

wet_type : str, optional

Type of wet material: ‘water’, ‘ice’
Returns:

humidity : Quantity

Relative humidity [%]
