Geometry helpers (pycraf.geometry)¶

Introduction¶

The geometry sub-package just offers some convenience functions for various geometrical calculations, e.g., in spherical coordinates.

See Also¶

  • Astropy Units and Quantities package, which is used extensively in pycraf.
  • Spherical coordinates

Reference/API¶

pycraf.geometry Package¶

Contains convenience functions for geometrical calculations on the sphere.

Functions¶

cart_to_sphere(x, y, z) Spherical coordinates from Cartesian representation.
great_circle_bearing(l1, b1, l2, b2) Great circle bearing between points (l1, b1) and (l2, b2).
sphere_to_cart(r, theta, phi) Spherical coordinates from Cartesian representation.
true_angular_distance(l1, b1, l2, b2) True angular distance between points (l1, b1) and (l2, b2).
